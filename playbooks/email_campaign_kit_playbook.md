# Email Campaign Kit Playbook

Use when building email templates, email campaign kits, subject lines, CTA banks, welcome sequences, affiliate promo sequences, or re-engagement sequences.

Positioning:
- Do not sell "30 AI emails".
- Sell a beginner-friendly campaign kit with campaign map, short editable templates, customization prompts, subject lines, checklist, planner, and examples.

Required use cases:
- Welcome
- Nurture/value
- Soft promo
- Affiliate promo
- Deadline/reminder
- Re-engagement

Required files:
- 00_Start_Here.md
- 01_7_Day_Campaign_Map.md
- 02_30_Short_Email_Templates.md
- 03_AI_Customization_Prompts.md
- 04_Pre_Send_Checklist.md
- 05_Subject_Line_Bank.md
- 06_Campaign_Planner.csv
- 07_Example_Campaign.md
- 08_License_Compliance.md

Compliance:
- Use real deadlines only.
- Avoid income guarantees.
- Avoid hype such as guaranteed sales, instant commissions, no work needed.
- Include placeholders for product, audience, pain, benefit, link, bonus, deadline.
