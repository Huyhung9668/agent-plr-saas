# License Risk Playbook

## Rule
When license terms are unclear, do not assume resale, PLR, agency, client, or whitelabel rights.

## Risk Labels
- Low: clear commercial/PLR rights and no restricted claims.
- Medium: license exists but has limits or vague redistribution language.
- High: no license, forum source, copied swipe, health/finance/income claims, or trademark risk.

## Required Warnings
- HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED for unclear rights.
- Do not grant whitelabel rights unless rights are explicit.
- Do not promise income, ranking, medical, legal, financial, conversion, or deliverability results.

## Safer Action
Use PLR as inspiration, then create original workflows, templates, checklists, examples, and assets.
