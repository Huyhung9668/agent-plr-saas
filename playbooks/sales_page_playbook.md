# Sales Page Playbook

Use when writing sales pages, offer angles, objections, proof substitutes, WarriorPlus listing, delivery copy, or FAQ.

Required flow:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- Problem
- Why raw AI/PLR is not enough
- Solution and mechanism
- What is inside
- How it works
- Bonuses
- Who it is for
- Who it is not for
- Price framing
- Risk reversal/refund language
- FAQ
- CTA

Mandatory objection:
"Can't I just ask AI to do this?"

Safe answer:
AI can create raw content, but buyers pay for workflow, structure, examples, checklists, planner, customization prompts, compliance guidance, and launch-ready packaging.

Compliance:
- No guaranteed income.
- No guaranteed conversion.
- No fake scarcity.
- No license claims unless rights are explicit.
