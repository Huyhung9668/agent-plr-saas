# JV Manager Playbook

Use when building JV pages, affiliate swipes, outreach, prospect trackers, affiliate tiers, launch calendar, or review access.

JV pack must include:
- JV page angle
- Product summary
- Buyer avatar
- Why promote this
- FE/OTO funnel map
- Commission recommendation
- Review access message
- Affiliate link placeholder
- 3 email swipes
- Social posts
- Bonus ideas
- Launch calendar
- Rules and compliance notes

Commission guidance:
- FE public: 50-75%.
- Approved JV FE: up to 90% if list quality is known.
- OTOs: usually 40-50%.
- Recurring: 30-50%.
- Do not give 90% across the whole funnel by default.

Outreach sequence:
- First contact: fit + product angle + review access.
- Follow-up: why it helps their audience.
- Final close: no-pressure future launch note.
