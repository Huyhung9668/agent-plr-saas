# PLR Rebrand Playbook

## Rule
Do not sell raw PLR unchanged. Treat PLR as research and raw material.

## Workflow
1. Identify buyer, niche, product type, and license clues.
2. Flag unclear rights as HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED.
3. Extract useful structure, not exact copy.
4. Reframe into a narrower promise and buyer.
5. Add original workflow, checklist, planner, examples, prompts, and launch assets.
6. Build FE, bump, OTO, JV pack, delivery page, and SaaS upgrade path.

## Quality Gate
- New product name.
- New positioning.
- New examples.
- New checklist/planner.
- License risk note.
- No copied sales claims.
