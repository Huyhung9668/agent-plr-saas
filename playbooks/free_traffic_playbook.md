# Free Traffic Playbook

## Rule
Free traffic plans must be practical and platform-safe. No spam, fake urgency, scraped DMs, or deceptive claims.

## Channels
- Facebook profile and groups.
- YouTube short tutorials.
- Blog/review posts.
- Quora/Medium answers.
- Email list follow-up.
- JV outreach.

## Soft Launch Plan
1. Share preview and ask for feedback.
2. Post one useful checklist or case study.
3. Contact small affiliates with review access.
4. Improve sales page from replies.
5. Run public launch only after offer clarity improves.
