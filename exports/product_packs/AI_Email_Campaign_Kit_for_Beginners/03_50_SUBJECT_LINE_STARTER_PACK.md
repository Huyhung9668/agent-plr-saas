Act as an email marketing assistant for beginners.

Audience: [Audience]
Topic: [Topic]
Offer: [Product]
Email goal: [Welcome/Nurture/Promo/Affiliate/Urgency/Re-engagement]
Main message: [Message]

Rules:
- Keep them short.
- Avoid hype.
- Do not mention guaranteed income.
- Make them clear and beginner-friendly.
- Give 5 curiosity lines, 5 direct lines, 5 benefit lines, and 5 soft reminder lines.
