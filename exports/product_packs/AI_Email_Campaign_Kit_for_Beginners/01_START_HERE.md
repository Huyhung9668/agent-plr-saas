# AI Email Campaign Kit for Beginners

## Start Here

This kit helps beginners write clearer email campaigns faster with simple templates, AI prompts, checklists, and campaign examples.

This is not a "copy-paste profit" system. It is a practical email planning and writing kit for people who want a simple structure instead of staring at a blank page.

## What Is Inside

| File | Purpose | Use It For |
|---|---|---|
| 30 Short Email Templates | Short beginner-friendly email templates | Welcome, nurture, promo, affiliate, urgency, re-engagement |
| 5 Done-For-You Campaigns | Complete campaign flows | Product launches, affiliate promos, lead magnets, reactivation |
| 50 Subject Line Starter Pack | Subject line ideas | Faster email writing and testing |
| Beginner Email Campaign Checklist | Pre-send checklist | Avoid unclear, risky, or confusing emails |
| Campaign Builder Sheet | Planning worksheet | Map audience, offer, sequence, CTA, deadline |
| Product Offer Blueprint | Vendor packaging guide | Turn the kit into a sellable WarriorPlus/Gumroad product |

## How To Use This Kit

1. Choose your campaign goal.
2. Pick one matching campaign or template.
3. Replace the placeholders with your niche, audience, offer, and link.
4. Use the customization prompt to rewrite the email in your own voice.
5. Check the email with the Beginner Email Campaign Checklist.
6. Send, track replies/clicks, and improve the next email.

## Placeholders

Use these placeholders throughout the templates:

```text
[Name]
[Product]
[Topic]
[Problem]
[Benefit]
[Link]
[Bonus]
[Deadline]
[Audience]
[Result]
[Offer]
[Reason]
[Action]
```

## Compliance Note

These templates are provided for educational and marketing planning purposes. Results depend on your audience, offer, traffic source, email list quality, and execution. No income or performance results are guaranteed. Always follow the email marketing rules and platform policies that apply to your business.

Avoid claims such as:

- Guaranteed sales
- Copy-paste profits
- Instant commissions
- Make money from every email
- No work needed

Use safer positioning such as:

- Beginner-friendly email templates
