# Product Offer Blueprint

## Product Name

AI Email Campaign Kit for Beginners

## Positioning

Write beginner-friendly email campaigns faster with short templates, AI prompts, checklists, and done-for-you campaign flows.

## Safe Promise

Create clearer welcome, nurture, promo, affiliate, urgency, and re-engagement emails without starting from a blank page.

## What This Product Is

This is a practical template and planning kit for beginners who want a simple structure for email marketing.

## What This Product Is Not

This is not a guaranteed income system, automated profit method, or advanced email marketing course.

## Target Buyers

| Buyer | Why They Want It |
|---|---|
| Beginner affiliate marketers | Need simple promo emails |
| Digital product sellers | Need launch and nurture emails |
| PLR buyers | Need templates to rebrand into a product |
| Small creators | Need list emails without overthinking |
| Freelancers/agencies | Need quick draft templates for client campaigns |
