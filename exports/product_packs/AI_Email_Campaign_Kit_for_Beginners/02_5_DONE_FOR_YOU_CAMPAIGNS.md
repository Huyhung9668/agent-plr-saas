# 5 Done-For-You Email Campaigns

## For AI Email Campaign Kit for Beginners

Use these as complete campaign flows. Each sequence is short, beginner-friendly, and designed to be customized with AI.

# Campaign 1: Welcome + Trust Campaign

## Goal

Welcome new subscribers, set expectations, and invite them to take one small action.

| Email | Purpose | CTA |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Welcome the reader | Visit the starter resource |
| 2 | Explain the simple process | Take one quick action |
| 3 | Teach one useful idea | Read/watch the next resource |
| 4 | Handle beginner overwhelm | Choose one small goal |
| 5 | Softly recommend the product | Check the product page |

[Link]

[Your Name]
