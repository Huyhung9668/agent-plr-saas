## Final Product Strategy Rule

The agent should not stop at content. It must answer:

1. Is this worth building?
2. Who buys it?
3. Why would they pay instead of asking AI?
4. What exact assets make it usable?
5. What workflow makes it easier?
6. What proof substitutes make it believable?
7. What compliance risks must be removed?
8. What sale page and funnel sell it?
9. What traffic and email follow-up support it?
10. Can it later become a SaaS/tool?
