Use one of six simple templates. Template A: One-page checklist with 12–20 action items. Template B: Worksheet with question prompts, blank fields, and example answer. Template C: Weekly planner with days, priorities, blockers, and review section. Template D: Client intake sheet with goals, current situation, next step, and consent note. Template E: Printable tracker with date, metric, action, result, note. Template F: Mini workbook with cover, instruction page, worksheet, checklist, next-step page. For Canva, use clean margins, readable font, no copyrighted characters, no brand logos, and no stock elements unless commercial-use rights are confirmed.
