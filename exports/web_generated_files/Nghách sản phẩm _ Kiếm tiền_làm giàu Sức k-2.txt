# AI Email Campaign Kit for Beginners  
## 30 Short English Email Templates — bản dùng được ngay

## 1. VERDICT

**NÊN LÀM**, nhưng không bán dưới dạng “30 email AI tạo”. Bản đó quá mỏng.  
Sản phẩm nên đóng gói thành **beginner email campaign kit**: có email mẫu, thứ tự dùng, prompt tùy chỉnh, checklist kiểm lỗi và planner.

Góc bán tốt hơn:

> **AI Email Campaign Kit for Beginners** helps beginner affiliate marketers and digital product sellers write simple welcome, nurture, promo, affiliate, deadline, and re-engagement emails without starting from a blank page.

# 7. AI CUSTOMIZATION PROMPT CHO BUYER

Đưa prompt này vào file `04_AI_Email_Customization_Prompts.pdf`.

```text
Act as a beginner-friendly email marketing assistant.

I will give you:
1. My niche
2. My target audience
3. My product or affiliate offer
4. The email template I want to customize

Your task:
Rewrite the email so it fits my audience and offer.

Rules:
- Keep the email short.
- Use simple English.
- Avoid hype and unrealistic claims.
- Do not promise income, sales, or guaranteed results.
- Keep the tone helpful and practical.
- Include one clear call to action.
- Keep all placeholders clear if information is missing.
- Add 3 subject line options.

My niche:
[Insert niche]

My target audience:
[Insert audience]

My offer:
[Insert product or affiliate offer]

Main problem my audience has:
[Insert problem]

Template to customize:
[Paste template here]
```

# 8. PRE-SEND CHECKLIST

Đưa phần này vào `05_Pre_Send_Email_Checklist.pdf`.

```text
Before sending your email, check:

[ ] Did I replace all placeholders?
[ ] Is the subject line clear and not misleading?
[ ] Is there only one main call to action?
[ ] Does the link work?
[ ] Did I avoid income guarantees or unrealistic claims?
[ ] If this is an affiliate email, did I include a disclosure?
[ ] If I mention a deadline, is it real?
[ ] Is the email short and easy to read?
[ ] Does the email match my audience and offer?
[ ] Did I send a test email to myself first?
```
