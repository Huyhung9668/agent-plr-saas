Use these prompts when the first draft feels weak.  
Fix generic angle: “Narrow this printable idea to 5 specific buyer groups with urgent, repeated use cases. Avoid broad audiences.”  
Fix thin pages: “Improve this worksheet so the user completes a real task, not just reads advice. Add fields, checkboxes, prompts, and completion criteria.”  
Fix risky claims: “Rewrite this product description to remove income, health, legal, platform approval, and guaranteed outcome claims.”  
Fix AI sameness: “Make this printable feel more original by adding a unique workflow, sequence, naming system, and practical examples.”  
Fix refund risk: “Identify why buyers may feel disappointed and suggest improvements before launch.”
