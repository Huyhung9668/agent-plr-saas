## Is this PLR?
UNKNOWN unless a separate license explicitly grants PLR/MRR/RR rights. Treat as personal/commercial-use instructional kit only until license is reviewed.

## Can I resell this exact kit?
Not unless resale rights are explicitly granted in your license.

## Can I use it to create my own printable products?
Yes, as a workflow and planning system, subject to checking rights for any images, fonts, Canva elements, AI outputs, or source PLR you use.

## Does it guarantee sales?
No.

## Can I use Canva?
Yes, but check Canva’s current license terms for any assets used.

## Can I use AI images?
Yes only after checking the AI tool’s commercial-use terms and avoiding trademark/copyright issues.
