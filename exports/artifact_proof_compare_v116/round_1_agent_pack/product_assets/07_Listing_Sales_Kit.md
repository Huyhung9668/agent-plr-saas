# Listing Sales Kit

## Marketplace title
Printable Prompt-to-Product Sprint: 7-Day AI Printable Product Kit

## Short description
A practical kit that helps creators turn AI-assisted printable ideas into organized product packs with prompts, examples, checklists, listing copy, and compliance notes.

## Benefit bullets
- Pick a printable niche without guessing blindly.
- Turn prompts into a structured product workflow.
- Use examples and quality checks to avoid raw AI output.
- Prepare listing/sales copy with safer claims.
- Package files clearly for buyers.

## Objection: Can I just ask AI?
Yes, AI can generate prompts. This kit adds sequence, quality checks, fix prompts, examples, packaging, sales copy structure, and compliance reminders.
