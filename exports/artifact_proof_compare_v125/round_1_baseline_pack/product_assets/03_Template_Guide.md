A strong printable template is simple, repeatable, and easy to understand. Recommended structure: Page 1 cover, Page 2 quick start guide, Pages 3-8 core worksheets, Pages 9-10 trackers or planners, Page 11 review page, Page 12 bonus checklist. Use consistent margins, font sizes, and spacing. Keep page titles action-based: “Plan Your Week,” “Track Your Tasks,” “Review Your Progress.” For KDP interiors, check trim size, bleed, page count, and Amazon KDP content rules before uploading. For PLR-style resale, create a clear license file and avoid using third-party graphics unless their license allows commercial redistribution. When unsure, replace assets with your own designs.
