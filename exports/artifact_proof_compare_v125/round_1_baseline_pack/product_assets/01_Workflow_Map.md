The workflow has seven steps. Step 1: pick one buyer and one use case. Step 2: generate a printable concept using the prompt library. Step 3: create the page structure: cover idea, instructions, worksheets, trackers, and bonus page. Step 4: edit for clarity, originality, and usefulness. Step 5: format in Canva, Google Docs, PowerPoint, Affinity, or similar tools. Step 6: run the quality checklist for spelling, page size, margins, commercial claims, and duplicate content risk. Step 7: package the product with a listing title, description, mockup, delivery file, license note, and support FAQ. Repeat only after one product passes the launch audit.
