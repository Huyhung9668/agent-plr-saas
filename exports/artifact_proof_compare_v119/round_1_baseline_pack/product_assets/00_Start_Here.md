Welcome to **Tiny Niche Printable Sprint** by Example Studio. This pack helps you create a small printable product from one narrow buyer problem instead of building a giant unfocused bundle. The goal is simple: pick a niche, generate printable content with AI, review it manually, format it cleanly, and prepare a basic sales/listing kit.

Recommended first product: a 10-page printable planner, checklist, tracker, or workbook for one audience. Example: “7-Day Declutter Tracker for Busy Moms” or “Client Onboarding Checklist for New Freelancers.”

Use this order: workflow map, prompt library, template guide, examples, quality checklist, fix prompts, listing kit, license/compliance. Do not skip manual review.
