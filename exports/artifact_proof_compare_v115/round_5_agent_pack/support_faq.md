# Support FAQ

Q: Is this a Canva template pack?
A: No. It is an implementation kit for creating printable lead magnets. You can design outputs in Canva or another tool.

Q: Can I resell the generated printables?
A: Only if your source materials, images, fonts, and platform terms allow it. HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED.

Q: Does this guarantee sales or subscribers?
A: No. It helps create assets, not guaranteed results.

Q: Can AI do this for free?
A: AI can draft. This kit adds workflow, examples, QC, fix prompts, and deployment structure.

Q: Is this KDP-ready?
A: It can help plan printable interiors, but KDP formatting, rights, and upload compliance must be checked separately.

Q: I have PLR. Can I use it?
A: Yes as research/outline if rights are unclear. Do not copy or resell unless license explicitly permits.
