# refund_risk.md

Refund risk is medium if the sales copy overpromises. Buyers in AI and PLR markets may expect fast money, done-for-you assets, or instant approval. Reduce risk by being clear: this is a workflow kit, not a guaranteed income system. It does not include verified image rights, KDP approval, buyer proof, sales data, or live payment proof.

The safest promise is: “Create a focused printable coloring page product plan with prompts, templates, quality checks, and listing copy.” Avoid: “make money today,” “guaranteed bestseller,” “instant KDP profits,” or “100% approved.” Include support instructions and a fair issue-based refund policy. Good onboarding also reduces refunds: tell buyers exactly which file to open first and what outcome to complete in 60 minutes.
