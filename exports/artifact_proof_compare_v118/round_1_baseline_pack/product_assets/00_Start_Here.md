Welcome to **AI Coloring Page Niche Sprint Kit** by Example Studio. This product helps you turn a broad printable idea into a focused coloring page pack concept, prompt set, quality checklist, listing description, and simple sales angle. It is useful for KDP creators, Etsy-style printable sellers, PLR vendors, and marketers who want a small, fast product rather than a giant unfocused bundle.

Start with one niche only. Good beginner niches include cozy animals, simple mindfulness patterns, seasonal classroom pages, cute food characters, and beginner mandalas. Avoid trademarked characters, celebrity names, brand names, copyrighted franchises, and medical or therapeutic claims.

Recommended first project: create a 20-page black-and-white printable coloring pack for adults or kids, then test it as a small digital download or lead magnet before building a full KDP book.
