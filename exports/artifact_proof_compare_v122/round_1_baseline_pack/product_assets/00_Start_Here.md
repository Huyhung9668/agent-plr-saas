Welcome to **AI Printable Niche Sprint Kit** by Example Studio. This product helps you create small printable product concepts such as planners, trackers, journals, worksheet packs, and low-content KDP interiors using a repeatable AI workflow. Start with one niche, one buyer, and one simple outcome. Do not build a giant bundle first. Your first goal is to create a 10–20 page printable concept, test the positioning, and improve it from feedback. Use the prompts as drafting tools, not final truth. Always review originality, formatting, spelling, rights, and marketplace rules before publishing or reselling any output.
