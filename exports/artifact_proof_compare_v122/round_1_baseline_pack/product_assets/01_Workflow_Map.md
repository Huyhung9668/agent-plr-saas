Use this workflow in order. Step 1: choose a narrow buyer, such as busy teachers, new Etsy sellers, homeschool parents, coaches, or productivity-focused creators. Step 2: select one printable format: checklist, planner, tracker, workbook, journal, or worksheet set. Step 3: define the promise: help the buyer complete one practical task faster. Step 4: generate page ideas with the prompt library. Step 5: create sample pages in Canva, Google Docs, PowerPoint, or Affinity. Step 6: run the quality checklist. Step 7: write a simple listing. Step 8: test with a small audience before scaling.
