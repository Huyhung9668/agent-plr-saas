Use this workflow in order. Step 1: Pick a narrow buyer, such as busy moms, Etsy printable sellers, teachers, coaches, or beginner KDP publishers. Step 2: Pick one outcome, such as weekly meal planning, reading tracking, client onboarding, or student practice. Step 3: Generate page ideas with the prompt library. Step 4: build pages in Canva, Google Docs, PowerPoint, Affinity, or similar software. Step 5: run the quality checklist. Step 6: create listing copy and preview images. Step 7: publish only after reviewing platform rules and licenses. Buyer test: ask ten target users which three pages they would actually use.
