# Prompt Library

## Prompt 1 - Niche Finder
Role: Bạn là product researcher cho printable/KDP.
Input: buyer=[...], platform=[KDP/Etsy/Gumroad], skill_level=[beginner].
Task: đề xuất 10 printable niches.
Constraints: tránh trademark, medical/legal claims, brand names.
Output: bảng gồm niche, buyer pain, product type, risk, easy first product.

## Prompt 2 - Product Blueprint
Role: Bạn là digital product architect.
Input: niche=[...], buyer=[...].
Task: tạo blueprint cho printable product pack.
Output: name, promise, 10 pages/assets, bonus, listing angle, compliance note.

## Prompt 3 - Page Content Generator
Role: Bạn là printable worksheet creator.
Input: product type=[planner/checklist/journal/kids worksheet], theme=[...].
Task: tạo nội dung cho 5 pages.
Constraints: simple, printable, non-medical, no copyrighted characters.
Output: page title, instructions, worksheet fields.

## Prompt 4 - Listing Writer
Role: Bạn là Etsy/KDP/Gumroad listing writer.
Input: product=[...], buyer=[...], deliverables=[...].
Task: viết title, subtitle, bullets, description, tags.
Constraints: no income guarantee, no fake scarcity.

## Prompt 5 - Quality Reviewer
Role: Bạn là QC editor.
Input: asset draft=[...].
Task: tìm lỗi trùng lặp, mơ hồ, quá hype, thiếu hướng dẫn, rủi ro license.
Output: pass/fail checklist + sửa cụ thể.
