### 6) Support FAQ — usable snippet

**Q: Does this guarantee sales on Etsy, KDP, Gumroad, or WarriorPlus?**  
No. The kit helps you create and organize a printable product concept. Sales depend on niche, quality, traffic, pricing, listing, and market demand.

**Q: Can I resell the prompts?**  
UNKNOWN unless the final license you attach explicitly allows resale/PLR/MRR. Default safer position: personal/commercial use for creating your own products, not reselling the kit itself.

**Q: Can I use Canva templates with this?**  
Yes, but you must verify Canva’s current license terms and any third-party elements/fonts before selling.

**Q: Is this Public Launch Ready?**  
No. This benchmark plan is raw chat only. It needs actual files, ZIP, placeholder check, delivery page, refund policy, and buyer test before public launch.

**Q: Can I use this for kids products?**  
Yes, with caution. Avoid educational guarantees, therapy claims, unsafe content, copyrighted characters, and age-inappropriate material.
