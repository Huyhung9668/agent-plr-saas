# 05_Quality_Checklist.md

Before selling, check every page. Is the buyer clear? Is the use case obvious in under ten seconds? Does each worksheet help the buyer complete a task, not just read information? Are fonts readable when printed? Are margins safe? Are spelling and grammar reviewed? Are page numbers consistent? Are instructions included? Are claims realistic? Is there any trademark, copyrighted artwork, or copied layout risk? Does the license page explain what buyers can and cannot do? Is the refund policy visible? Is delivery tested? Buyer test: ask three target users if they understand the product, would use it, and what page is missing.
