## 6. Support FAQ snippet

**Q: Will this guarantee KDP ranking or sales?**  
No. This kit helps you create and evaluate printable product drafts. It does not guarantee sales, profit, ranking, approval, or marketplace results.

**Q: Can I resell the prompts as PLR?**  
UNKNOWN unless the final license you provide explicitly allows it. Default recommendation: sell as personal/commercial-use implementation training, not resale rights.

**Q: Can I use Canva assets?**  
Only if your Canva account/license and specific assets allow your intended use. Check Canva terms before selling templates or design files.

**Q: Is this for kids products?**  
It can help create kids worksheet/coloring ideas, but avoid claims about learning improvement, therapy, diagnosis, or guaranteed child outcomes.

**Q: What if AI output looks bad?**  
Use the fix prompts and quality checklist. Do not publish low-quality, unreadable, distorted, or copied-looking output.
