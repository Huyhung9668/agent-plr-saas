# Baseline Codex 5.5 Output — Round 1

Constraint: no project agent, no skill markdown, no compressed brain, no project RAG folders.

## Product Idea
**AI Coloring Page Niche Pack Builder** — a practical PLR/KDP printable kit for creators who want a structured pack instead of a raw prompt list.

## Buyer
Solo PLR sellers, KDP beginners, Etsy printable creators, coaches building lead magnets, and WarriorPlus vendors who need a fast front-end offer.

## Pain
They can generate ideas with AI, but they struggle to package them into usable interiors, checklists, examples, sales material, license notes, and a delivery-ready bundle.

## Safe Promise
Create a structured printable/KDP product pack faster, with clear prompts, examples, checklists, and compliant launch materials. No income or ranking guarantee.

## Product Blueprint
- `00_Start_Here.md`: buyer orientation and 10-minute first win.
- `01_Workflow_Map.md`: choose niche, generate asset, review, package, list.
- `02_Prompt_Library.md`: niche research, interior generation, listing copy, QA prompts.
- `03_Template_Guide.md`: Canva/KDP formatting notes and layout guidance.
- `04_Example_Outputs.md`: sample pages, prompts, and before/after improvements.
- `05_Quality_Checklist.md`: originality, readability, page consistency, license checks.
- `06_Fix_Prompts.md`: repair generic, repetitive, unsafe, or malformed output.
- `07_Listing_Sales_Kit.md`: sales page outline, WarriorPlus listing, affiliate angles.
- `08_License_Compliance.md`: copyright, trademark, Canva, font, KDP, PLR rules.
- `README.md`: support path, delivery notes, version, disclaimer.

## Product Assets Outline
Includes detailed prompt categories, a quick-start workflow, sample output standards, a QA checklist, listing copy framework, and compliance notes. It outlines assets well but does not create every full file.

## Sales Page Outline
Headline, subheadline, problem, why raw AI is not enough, solution, what is inside, how it works, who it is for/not for, FAQ, license note, CTA, disclaimer.

## WarriorPlus Listing
- FE price: $17–$27
- Order bump: Canva template pack or example output vault, $9–$17
- OTO1: DFY niche bundle expansion, $37–$67
- OTO2: reseller/commercial expansion with extra swipes, $97+
- Commission: 50% FE, 40% OTO, manual affiliate approval.

## JV Pack Outline
JV invite, 5 email swipe topics, 5 social post angles, promo rules, compliance notes, prohibited income claims.

## License / Compliance Note
Avoid trademarks, copyrighted characters, celebrity names, lyrics, brand phrases, guaranteed income/rank claims, therapy/health claims, and unsupported kids education guarantees. Canva/font/AI assets must allow commercial use.

## Buyer Test
Buyer likely understands the product if Start Here exists. Risk: baseline output remains an outline and may feel incomplete without real files, examples, and export proof.

## AI Replace Risk
Medium. Reduced by workflow, checklists, examples, and fix prompts, but still vulnerable if only prompts are delivered.

## Refund Risk
Medium. Main risks: not enough finished assets, unclear license, no support FAQ, no examples.

## Launch Readiness Score
Soft launch concept only: 68/100. It needs real files, ZIP export, buyer test, prompt output test, and public launch audit.
