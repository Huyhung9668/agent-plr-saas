# 03_Template_Guide.md

Recommended printable structure: cover page, usage guide, core tracker pages, planning pages, review pages, and bonus checklist. For KDP interiors, keep margins clean, avoid tiny text, and use black-and-white friendly layouts unless color is essential. For printable PDFs, include US Letter and A4 if possible. For PLR-style buyers, add editable Canva or PowerPoint source files only if your license permits resale and editing. Strong pages have a clear action: write, check, track, plan, compare, or review. Weak pages are decorative filler. Use repeatable grids, simple prompts, and enough white space for handwriting.

# 05_Quality_Checklist.md

Before selling, review every page. Is the buyer clear? Does each page help complete a real task? Are there duplicate pages with only minor title changes? Is the text readable when printed? Are margins safe for binding? Are there spelling errors? Does the PDF export correctly? Are source files included only when allowed? Does the product avoid income guarantees, medical advice, legal advice, and copyrighted brand misuse? AI replace risk: medium. Generic printable ideas are easy to copy. Reduce risk by adding niche-specific workflows, examples, instructions, checklists, and editable layouts that save time for a specific buyer.
