# Usage Summary: AI PLR Rebrand Kit

## Safe Use
- Sell newly created original assets.
- Use unclear PLR as research only.
- Keep license/compliance notes in delivery.

## Do Not
- Do not grant PLR/whitelabel rights for third-party files unless rights are explicit.
- Do not copy sales pages, swipes, or claims from source PLR.
- Do not promise income or guaranteed launch results.

## Human Review Required
Required for any source file with unclear license, forum origin, missing purchase record, or copied swipe/sales copy.
