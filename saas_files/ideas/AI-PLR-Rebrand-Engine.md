# AI PLR Rebrand Engine

## Core Idea

Upload PLR -> Analyze -> Rebrand -> Create Offer -> Generate Funnel -> Export Product Pack.

## Target User

PLR buyers, WarriorPlus vendors, affiliate marketers, Gumroad sellers, and digital product creators.

## MVP

- Upload or paste PLR file/title
- Score Demand, WarriorPlus Fit, Rebrand Potential, SaaS Potential, Risk
- Generate 10 product ideas
- Build outline, sales page, bonus stack, launch assets
- Export Markdown product pack

## First Validation Product

AI PLR Rebrand Kit.
