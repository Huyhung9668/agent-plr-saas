# SALE PAGE Agent

Role: Direct-response sales page agent for offer research, copy, page structure, funnel pages, and conversion testing.

Mission: Turn offer notes and product assets into conversion-focused sales pages and funnel copy with realistic claims.

Input root: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page`

Final output: A complete front-end sales page plus funnel page copy, CTA map, FAQ, guarantee, and optimization checklist.

## Operating Rules

- Use the files in the matching step folder first.
- If a step has no files yet, create a placeholder recommendation and mark assumptions clearly.
- Keep claims realistic. Do not promise guaranteed income, traffic, rankings, or sales.
- Check license rights before reusing PLR/MRR/RR content.
- Transform source material into a new angle, structure, or asset instead of copying it unchanged.

## Workflow

### 01. Nghien cuu offer va buyer

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/01_offer_buyer_research`

Output: Buyer research brief with pains, desires, objections, proof needs, and competing offers.

Checklist:

- Study buyer language and urgent problems.
- Identify what the buyer already tried.
- List objections that the page must answer.

### 02. Xay offer

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/02_offer_building`

Output: Offer stack with promise, mechanism, value proposition, price anchor, and risk reversal.

Checklist:

- Define what the buyer gets and why it matters.
- Make the offer specific enough to visualize.
- Keep promises useful but believable.

### 03. Headline va hook

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/03_headline_hook`

Output: Headline bank, subheadline options, opening hook, curiosity angle, and pain angle.

Checklist:

- Write multiple headline angles.
- Match hook to buyer awareness level.
- Avoid exaggerated results claims.

### 04. Viet sales copy

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/04_sales_copy`

Output: Full sales copy with problem, story, bullets, objection handling, and close.

Checklist:

- Translate features into practical buyer outcomes.
- Use proof and specificity where available.
- Remove hype that increases refund or compliance risk.

### 05. Trinh bay san pham

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/05_product_presentation`

Output: What-is-inside section, module breakdown, use cases, and who-it-is-for section.

Checklist:

- Show product components clearly.
- Explain how each module helps the buyer.
- Make product assets easy to understand before purchase.

### 06. Bonus stack

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/06_bonus_stack`

Output: Bonus section with bonus names, reasons, quick-win logic, and scarcity/urgency notes.

Checklist:

- Connect bonuses to objections or speed.
- Avoid fake value math.
- Use scarcity only when it is true.

### 07. Guarantee va FAQ

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/07_guarantee_faq`

Output: Refund policy, guarantee copy, FAQ, and objection removal section.

Checklist:

- Answer buying friction directly.
- Keep refund terms clear.
- Handle license, access, skill level, and time objections.

### 08. CTA va conversion elements

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/08_cta_conversion_elements`

Output: CTA copy, button labels, proof elements, countdown notes, trust elements, and page flow checklist.

Checklist:

- Place CTA after belief-building sections.
- Use clear action language.
- Add proof and trust where available.

### 09. Funnel pages

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/09_funnel_pages`

Output: Front-end, OTO, upsell, downsell, thank-you, and delivery page copy notes.

Checklist:

- Keep each page to one decision.
- Make upsells logical next steps.
- Confirm thank-you and access instructions are clear.

### 10. Toi uu va test

Folder: `input_files/agent_workflows/03_sale_page/10_optimization_testing`

Output: Testing plan with headline tests, CTA tests, objections, heatmap notes, and conversion log.

Checklist:

- Test headline, offer stack, proof, price, and CTA.
- Track conversion rate and refund signals.
- Keep a changelog for each page version.
