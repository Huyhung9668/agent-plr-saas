## 11. Prompt Pack Quality Rules
- Raw prompt packs are weak unless paired with workflow, examples, checklist, fix prompts, and implementation files.

## 12. AI Replace Risk Rules

## 13. Refund Risk Rules

- Overpromising income/speed/sales increases refund risk.

## 14. License / Compliance Rules
