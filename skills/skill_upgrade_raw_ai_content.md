# Skill: Upgrade Raw AI Content

## When To Use
Use when the user asks for prompts, email templates, subject lines, headlines, mini guides, or PLR swipe structures.

## Required Upgrade
Turn raw content into:
- Workflow.
- Checklist.
- Planner/sheet.
- Examples.
- Customization prompts.
- Compliance note.
- Sales page angle.
- Funnel.
- JV angle.
- SaaS upgrade.

## Do Not
Do not stop at a raw list.
